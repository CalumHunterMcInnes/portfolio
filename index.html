About Me

I am a recent graduate with a 2.1 in BSc Computing and Smart Devices from Liverpool John Moores University, skilled in Python, C, and C++. My academic journey has been inspired by a genuine interest in home automation, sensor networks, and embedded systems, reflecting the essence of my enthusiasm.

Beyond academic achievements, I bring a keen interest in building real-world projects that seamlessly integrate hardware and software components. My commitment to continuous learning is evident in my proficiency, highlighted by the creation of a personal homelab server using Linux-based systems and virtual environments. Specialized skills include expertise in wireless communication protocols such as MQTT and BLE, alongside a penchant for designing, developing, and testing sensor-based systems.

My vision extends to contributing meaningfully to the ever-evolving field of Internet of Things and the opportunities it brings. Having my dream of establishing a fully customised smart home setup, I am eager to bring this vision to fruition while contributing my skills and enthusiasm to a dynamic professional environment and exploring the boundless possibilities it holds for the future.
